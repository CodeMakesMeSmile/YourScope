Our Mission

At YourScope, we know that every student has the potential to succeed, and we want to do everything we can to make that happen. Our service provides students with a course planner, an events tracker, and an internal job board for students looking to find work early! We strive to make your high school experience as smooth and enjoyable as possible.


Perfect For Students and Employers


Our service provides employers with a platform to seek young and talented new workers, giving students an opportunity to gain valuable life skills and workplace experience before they even graduate high school. Employers can register a YourScope account and can share job postings on the network, reaching many talented people looking for work. With YourScope, searching for applicants has never been easier!


Partnering With Schools

YourScope partners with schools all across Ontario in order to ensure accurate information regarding offered courses and upcoming events are provided to students. When a school partners with YourScope, an admin account will be created for them which can be used to monitor and update offered courses and events.
